About Naichana Village

History, Demographics, Facilities & More

1. Location And Administration

  • Naichana (also known as Nehchana) is a village in Tehsil Bawal, District Rewari, Haryana. It spans about 5-6 kms from Bawal Town.
  • This village comes under Naichana Gram Panchayat and The Bawal Community Development Block.

2. Postal Code and Connectivity

  • The PIN Code for Naichana is 123501, same as Bawal and neighbouring villages and localities.
  • Well-connected with both private and public buses, with the nearest Railway Station (Bawal) about 8 kms away.

3. Demographics and Social Data

  • According to 2009-11 data:
  • Population: ~3000 individuals residing in about 500 households, with a gender split of 1651 males and 1349 females (Approx. Values).
  • Scheduled Castes account for around 15-16% of the total population.
  • Literacy stands higher than state average at ~83.9%, in which Males: 92.8% and Females: 73.4%.

4. Land Use and Infrastructure

  • The total area is roughly 546 hectares.
  • Of this:
  • 480 hectares is agricultural land — 260 ha irrigated (mainly via tubewells or directly from canals) and 220 ha left unirrigated.
  • 22 ha is non-agricultural, and 44 ha serves as postural and grazing lands for animals.

5. Educational Facilities

  • A notable institution: Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Naichana, established in 1992, provides co-educational, CBSE-board education from class 6-12, inclusive of residential facilities for students and teachers, library and computer labs.
  • A Government School (Haryana board, upper primary to secondary).

6. Healthcare Access

  • Within the village: a primary health sub-centre with one paramedical staff.
  • Nearby health facilities (5-10 kms away): Community health centre, Primary health centre, Maternity and child welfare centre, TB clinic, Dispensary, Veterinary hospital. More advanced care services lie farther than 10 kms.
